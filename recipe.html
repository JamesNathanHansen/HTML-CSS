Toasted Cheese Sandwich

This webpage shows the ingredients and steps to make a toasted cheese sandwich. It is the tastiest and easiest snack to make!

Ingredients:

  • Cheese
  • Butter
  • Bread
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Green Chilli
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Grate the cheese üßÄ
  2. Butter two slices of bread 🧈 🍞
  3. Slice the tomato, onion and green chilli 🍅 🧅 🌶
  4. Layer the cheese, sliced tomato, onion and chilli onto one of the slices of bread
  5. Grind the pepper and salt over the other ingredients 🧂
  6. Place the other slice on top
  7. Put a little butter in a pan over a medium to low heat
  8. Once the butter has melted, place the sandwich in the pan
  9. Toast each side till golden brown and the cheese has fully melted 🥪
  10. Slice diagonally and enjoy! 🤩

History:

The idea of grilling bread and cheese has been around for centuries. Roman cookbooks say to combine bread and cheese when eating dinner. In France, the Croque Monsieur (toasted bread, cheese, and ham) was first on menus in 1910. Otto Frederick Rohwedder designed a machine that would slice bread in 1927 and by 1933 bakeries were producing and selling more sliced than unsliced loaves of bread. During the Great Depression, people began eating open-faced grilled cheese sandwiches as a cost-effective meal. In 1916, James L. Kraft applied for a patent to make processed cheese. By 1949, Kraft Foods developed pre-packed sliced of cheese that were shelf-stable.
